Aavartan

AAVARTAN is the National Level Techno-Management Fest of NIT Raipur comprising of many technical & management events, workshops, guest lectures, tech shows and much more bringing out the best of students in technical and entrepreneurial domain. AAVARTAN, the name itself suggests INNOVATION.

VIGYAAN

Vigyaan- The National Level Science Exhibition conducted during the biggest technical two day extravaganza of Central India AAVARTAN by NIT Raipur. Vigyaan serves as the centre of attraction for various people visiting the college during the Techno Management Fest.
