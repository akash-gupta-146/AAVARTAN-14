Wall Street

Still the mistry

Overview

Wall Street refers to the financial district of New York. Over time, the term has become a metonym for the financial markets as a whole.

It is here that millionaires are born, billionaires go broke and things become topsy-turvy in a matter of seconds. In a scenario as unpredictable as such, even the toughest are liable to crumble.

And when all the action is in town, imagination will go haywire thinking of it.

Wall Street, a mock stock exchange, an Event to test your mettle and drive you to the very edge of your wits.This is a good experience for everyone who has ever dreamt of being rich or bidding high. Dive into the market world and experience an adrenaline gushing show all day long. But beware, you may end up in bottom less pit and go broke. Participants, provided with virtual money, will invest it by buying stocks of companies, earn profit by selling stocks in the virtual stock market, to maximize their profits.

Discription

The participants will be provided with certain initial amount of virtual money, which they will be using for buying virtual stocks. The stock market details (stock prices, etc.) for each virtual company will be generated and modified by the servers, details of which will be shown through a projector. Participants can sell the stocks they bought, accordingly with the current prices of the stock. In this way a participantï¿½s profit value will change, a participant may even end up in some loss. At the end of each day the participant with maximum profit will be declared the winner. Judgment Criteria The maximum profit gainer for each will be selected based on their profits.

Rules

Team Composition: This is a single participation event.

This event is going on for 2 Days of the fest.

Rules of the Competition:

The game will be played for 5 hours daily i.e. from 12 am to 5pm.

Everybody will be given a starting amount of one lakh in the start.

Each team will have to buy their shares from a broker.

The list and details of IPOs will be told on the spot.
