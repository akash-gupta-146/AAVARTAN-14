ROBOWAR

Battle of future Gladiators

Overview

In ancient times they were called GLADIATORS; in modern world we call them SOLDIERS. Times might have changed but the urge to battle and win to be crowned the best is still intrinsic in humans. Aavartan gives you the chance to make a mechanical warrior of yourself and fight with it to prove your supremacy.

Discription’ The event will be held in 2 rounds. Only Registered teams will be allowed to participate in the event. Each team can consist of maximum 5 participants. Round 1: This round will be a one-on-one round. Participants have to bring their own robots to fight against the other robots. Each match will be of maximum 5 minutes until one participant immobilizes the other participant for atleast 30secs. Attack and aggression of your robot helps you gain more points. Points for attack, aggression, strategy and control will be noted by the coordinators. Winners of a match will be decided by their points scored. Round 2: This round is also a one-on-one round. Slight changes will be done in the arena to make the war a bit difficult. Participants will be given extra points for efficient use of the arena. Each match will be of maximum 3 minutes before one participant immobilizes the other participant. Points will be noted by the coordinators. Winners of a match will be decided by their points scored.

Rules


• The machine should fit in a box of dimension 700 mm x 700 mm x 750 mm (l x b x h). The external device used to control the machine is not included in the size constraint.
• The machine should not exceed 40 kg of weight. Weight of external power source (batteries and adaptors)and the remote controller will not be counted.
• The machine can be controlled wirelessly or with wires. The wires should be properly insulated. Teams are suggested to use only rated wires.
• Robots can have any kind of magnetic weapons, cutters, flippers, saws, lifting devices, spinning hammers etc. as weapons with following exceptions and limitations:
a. Liquid projectiles.
b. Any kind of inflammable liquid.
c. Flame-based weapons.
d. Any kind of explosive or intentionally ignited solid or potentially ignitable solid.
e. Nets, tape, glue, or any other entanglement device.
f. High power magnets or electromagnets.
g. Radio jamming, tazers, tesla coils, or any other high-voltage device.
h. Tethered or un-tethered projectiles.
• In no case should the arena be damaged by any bot.
• Maximum input voltage allowed is 24 volt D.C.
• A team may consist of at max 5 members.
