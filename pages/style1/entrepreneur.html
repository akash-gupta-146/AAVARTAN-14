The Entrep Fair

Sales Exhibition

Overview

This is an event to enhance Creativity, Marketing, Presentation, Management, Teamwork & Entrepreneurship skills of the participants.

In the event, the students who has got talent in any field can exhibit them and put the products they made for sale for example - cooking, painting, pottery, photography, singing, playing musical instruments (by making CDs), even your marketing, management skills (by selling any product they want) and any other skills.

FIRST ROUND : ABSTRACT SUBMISSION

1. Abstract must contain the description and specialties of your product, how you are planning it or selling it, how much or how many products you think you can sell in the given time period of 3 hours & the budget of your plan.

2. Selection will be done based on the feasible budgets, uniqueness of the product and creativity in your marketing plan.

3. Maximum SIX (6) TEAMS will be qualified for the second round.

4. Abstract submission last date - 8th October, 2014. MAIL your abstracts at - theentrepreneurfair@gmail.com

SECOND ROUND : SETTING UP THE STALLS

1. This round actually concerns the quality of your product. In this round, teams will have to setup their stalls in the college premises for 3 hours at the time of fest.

2. Every customer will be given only a coupon (only one), free of cost, with which they can buy any one and only one of the product from our Entrepreneur Fair.

3. The participants should attract the customers with their marketing skills and must manage to make customers buy the products. All the things required to set up the stall must be brought by the participants themselves.

4. At the end of third hour, the team with highest number of coupons will be declared as the winner.

The winners will be awarded with the prize money !!!

Rules

1. The participants have to submit their marketing plan in the form of an abstract on or before 8th of October (08/10/2014).

2. A team can have a maximum of three members.

3. Any way of involving or accepting money can lead to disqualification in the second round.

4. The budget must be feasible.

5. All the things require to set up the stall must be brought by the participants in the second round. Make your B-plan (Abstract) and mail it to us - theentrepreneurfair@gmail.com
