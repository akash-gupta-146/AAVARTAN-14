Technomic

History & Present

Overview

However intelligent or technically sound, a student should also have sufficient knowledge about history as well as current affairs about the things he will deal in his real career like science, business, environment and society. This is an event to sharpen your knowledge, make you up to date, and calculate your Techonomy Quotient.

Discrption

A Science-Tech, Business, Environment & General Awareness quiz which will comprise of three rounds.

Round 1

Written Test (60 Questions-45 minutes) . General Knowledge and quiz on science and business.

Round 2

For top 30 Teams: Audio Visual Round. 5 MCQ+5 Questions Without Options +5 Audio Visual (Everything including MCQ being displayed on Projector)

Round 3

For top 6 Teams, Grand Finale (Audio Visual, Rapid Fire, Optional theme rounds)

Judging criteria:

Marks obtained in question paper for round 1.

Maximum marks scored in rounds 2 and 3.

Rules

1. Each team should compulsorily consist of two members.

2. A Team may comprise of students from same/different college, branch and semester.

3. Teams will be forwarded to further round only by maximum marks gained in that round by answering correctly.
