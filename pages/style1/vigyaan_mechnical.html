Vigyaan

Mechanical

Problem Statements

1. Design a thermal microturbine (power generation system) for use in rural areas with the help of waste materials like paddy husk, dry leaves, bagasse etc

2. Using cam (camshaft), pump motor, pipe and nozzle, design a model such that the projectile range of water jet from nozzle can be maximised. Hint- Rise in pressure of water jet due to interruption by cam can cause change in its projectile range

3. Build a working model of robotic arm that can do under water operations like welding, cutting and machining at high depths of water. You may use water resistive adhesives to provide insulation to the motors from water.

4. Develop an automatic emergency breaking system for a car. Hint-The system should have sensors to check the proximity of the nearest object and have decision making capability whether or not to automatically apply the emergency brakes.

5. Use of microwave technology for welding of materials like metals, plastics, alloys etc. Hint-Description of how the wave is to be generated, focused and maintained without overheating the workplace and its components.

Rules

A team can choose only one problem statement and in case a team opts for technical domain, they have to choose it of their department only.
Team size can be maximum of 3 members.
All participating teams have to prepare an abstract describing the solution and submit it in IEEE format as a PDF file as given in sample abstract by 21/09/2014, 11PM at abstract@aavartan.in .
Along with abstract PDF, you also have to submit another PDF file with the Team details( Name, College, Branch, Sem, Contact, Email ID) of all the team members.
Late Submission won't be entertained."
