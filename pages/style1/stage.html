Overview

"SHOW US WHAT YOU GOT. TIME TO GET MAD ABOUT ADS"

If you have the creative bent, power of packaging and presentation, drama, then welcome to this event, which challenges your imagination to think creative and big.

This is an event for all those who have the ability to cast a spell on buyers. It is about putting the very heartthrobs of a business into type, music, dance, comedy, action together with a social message.

Rules

1. Participants will have to register before a particular date.

2. Maximum number of members in a team will be 10.

3. Company name or its product for which they have to perform will be given before 2 days of event.

4. Skit should be of maximum 10 minutes.

5. They can use banners and instruments which support their act.

6. They can include music, dance, comedy, action, thriller, horror, emotions everything that

7. can entertain the audience

8. Language of the act should be English, Hindi or both.

9. Reflection of a social message is a must.

10. Abusive or double meaning language is not allowed.

11. Final decision of winner will be decided by Judges.
